Overview

About Filmwaker × IVGO

Filmwaker is a creative production studio focused on cinematic video, advanced 3D workflows and live-event visual design. This offer has been built specifically for IVGO, assuming a long-term collaboration over multiple concerts, where the value grows concert after concert.

Our approach is to treat each IVGO concert like an evolving chapter in the same universe: same visual language, tailored to each specific videogame in focus. The longer the partnership, the stronger and more recognisable the visual identity becomes.

Long-term collaboration

All prices shown are per concert and considered without VAT and assume a recurring collaboration. This allows us to optimise workflows, reuse pipelines and visual systems, and maintain high quality while keeping costs stable.

Production Packages

Three levels, one cohesive visual strategy

Each level can be combined with the others. Level 2 is an upgrade to Level 1; Level 3 focuses on LED wall visuals during the live show.

Level 1

Base Mission

Recording & basic post-production

Long-term price €3,000 / concert
Standalone price €5,000 / concert
  • • Filmwaker crew in attendance (min. 3 operators)
  • • Multi-camera coverage of the entire concert
  • • Standard professional editing & color correction
  • • Export of social-ready video versions

External equipment rentals are not included. The reduced long-term price reflects efficiency with the same client, recurring format and shared workflows.

Level 2

Expansion Pack

3D integration & advanced editing

Long-term price €4,250–€6,500 / concert
Standalone price €5,500-€8,500 / concert
  • • Everything in Level 1 Pack
  • • 3D asset creation tied to the videogame theme
  • • Motion graphics and stylised transitions
  • • Advanced compositing, VFX and cinematic pacing

This level becomes more cost-effective after the first concert, as visual language and assets can be reused and evolved show after show.

Level 3

Ultimate Visual Pack

LED wall design for full immersion

Long-term price €9,500–€13,500 / concert
Standalone price €13,000–€16,000 / concert
  • • Approx. 90 minutes of LED wall loops & animations
  • • Visual identity aligned with the featured videogame
  • • Sync with music cues and conductor notes
  • • Delivery in ready-to-run formats for the venue

Ideal for concerts that want the stage itself to become part of the videogame universe the orchestra is performing.
